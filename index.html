About Me

As a seasoned property specialist, I am passionate about delivering precise and insightful real estate solutions. In my current role as a Chartered Valuation Surveyor with the Cayman Islands Government, I provide independent, impartial valuation and professional property advice across the core Government sector, particularly where public money or public functions are involved. My expertise spans property valuation, compulsory purchase and compensation, and property tax assessments.

I am also an Adjunct Lecturer at the University College of the Cayman Islands and lead research on topics such as inflation-hedging properties, automated valuation models, and housing affordability. I am committed to using data-driven insights to support evidence-based decision-making in real estate and land policy. As a Member of the Royal Institution of Chartered Surveyors (MRICS) and a registered valuer, I uphold the highest standards of professional integrity and excellence.
